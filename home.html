About

Perspective includes design work from 14 undergraduate designers completing a BFA in graphic design and will be live on Tuesday, November 24, 2020. Perspective expresses our power to break free from the formal constraints of design. ... View our exhibition at www.perspectivebfa.com, that goes live on Nov 24. "place holder"

The Florida Atlantic University 2020 Bachelor of Fine Arts class will be hosting their exhibition at the end of the fall semester. This exhibition will showcase a selection of our most accomplished works within design practices. The purpose of this exhibition is to present ourselves as professional designers ready-for-hire and to further our careers or grad studies. Due to the pandemic, we are refocusing our energy from the traditional physical exhibition to gaining the attention of design professionals with an online showcase that will compiles all of our best work. Nevertheless, We are reaching out to individuals and businesses for support in the form of monetary donations or services and products to help us achieve our goals. Provided donations will assist in the cost of printing and mailing invitations, as well as the promotion of our exhibition. Donations of any size and form will be acknowledged on our website. Donations in the value of $100 will be listed on our website as well as social media platforms. Generous donations of $500 or more will feature your personal or business name/logo on our website, social media platforms, and printed invitations. The printed invitations are mailed to over 1250 creative professionals in South Florida. The university is non-profit and all donations in the form of checks are tax-deductible. We request that donations be made prior to October 15 to receive recognition on the printed invitation. Donations thereon after will be greatly appreciated as well. Please make checks payable to “FAU Foundations” and mail to: Florida Atlantic University Department of Visual Arts and Art History, LA323 3200 College Avenue Davie, Florida Attention: Professor Cunningham If you have any questions please contact class president Jessica Reyes at jreyes2019@fau.edu. We thank you greatly for your consideration and generosity during these unprecedented times.
